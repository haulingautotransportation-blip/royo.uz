    How RoYo Works

    1. Shipper posts a load

    Origin, destination, cargo type, weight, preferred timing – all entered in less than 30 seconds through the web app.

    2. Drivers receive offers

    Verified drivers see available loads, suggested rates, and optimal routes in one place instead of ten different chats.

    3. AI optimizes the trip

    RoYo recommends price, route, and backhaul loads to cut empty miles and increase earnings per kilometer.

    Key Features

    Freight Marketplace

    One interface where shippers, drivers, and fleet owners meet. Filter by route, cargo type, vehicle, and price.

    Real-time Tracking

    See every truck on the map, live status of each shipment, estimated arrival times, and exception alerts.

    AI Routing & Pricing

    RoYo suggests the best price and route based on fuel, demand, border delays, and historical performance.

    Fleet & Driver Panel

    Manage multiple trucks, assign trips, monitor utilization, and track performance from a simple dashboard.

    Digital Documents

    Store CMR, invoices, contracts, and delivery confirmations in one place. No more lost papers or WhatsApp chaos.

    Ready for Mobile

    The same backend will power native iOS & Android apps for drivers and shippers once the web MVP is stable.
